The Process

We like to keep things simple. This step by step process outlines your journey to creating your website from beginning to end.

01

Consultation

First, we run through ideas, site requirements, and budget.

02

Proposal

After that, we propose a contract, ironing out any outstanding details.

03

Development

We then begin building your site, with check-ins occurring weekly.

04

Delivery

Lastly, we transfer the site and all associated files into your possession.
